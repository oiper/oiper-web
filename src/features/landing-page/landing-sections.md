## 2. How OiPer Works

A simple workflow designed for speed.

1. Hold a global hotkey
2. Speak
3. Release the hotkey

Your speech is instantly transcribed and inserted into the active application.

No switching apps. No waiting.

## 3. Core Features

**Global Hotkey Recording**
Start recording from anywhere.

**Instant Text Injection**
Text appears directly in the app you are using.

**Local Transcription**

**Optional AI Text Cleanup**
Improve formatting or grammar using local or online models.

**Flexible Models & Backends**
